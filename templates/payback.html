Albion Online payback times for house upgrade

{% endblock %} {% block body %}

Returns the number of days to payback the cost of upgrading a house and furniture for 3 labourers using current city prices.
Uses the calculation: (cost of upgrading house and furniture for 3 labourers - sale of old furniture) / ((profit per journal after upgrade - profit per journal before upgrade)*3)
Note that the labourer limit per house is t2: 1, t3 & t4: 2, t5 and up: 3. This is taken into account in the calculation.
City: Selects the city market for buying the full journal and selling the labourer output materials and empty journal.
House tier: The current tier of the house and furniture inside. Assumes all applicable trophies are used excluding sharks and spyglasses.

Labourers cannot work journals higher than their own tier.
The sales include the premium tax of 4.5%, non-premium members can expect a lower profit than shown after selling their labourer's output on the market.
The cell is left blank if one of the journals or unenchanted materials for that journal has no current price on the city market.
Daily journal profit comes from the journal profitpage
